Orthodontics & Braces

Straighten your teeth with our advanced orthodontic solutions including traditional braces and modern clear aligners.

Treatment Process

  1. Initial Consultation: Comprehensive examination and 3D imaging
  2. Treatment Planning: Customized plan with timeline and cost
  3. Brace Installation: Professional application of selected braces
  4. Regular Adjustments: Monthly appointments for adjustments
  5. Retention: Post-treatment retention plan

Dental Implants

Replace missing teeth with natural-looking, permanent dental implants for optimal functionality and aesthetics.

Treatment Process

  1. Assessment: CBCT imaging and bone evaluation
  2. Bone Grafting (if needed): Prepare sufficient bone structure
  3. Implant Placement: Surgical insertion of titanium implant
  4. Osseointegration: 3-6 months for implant to fuse with bone
  5. Crown Placement: Custom crown attached to implant

Duration & Timeline

1-2 hours

Implant placement surgery

3-6 months

Osseointegration period

2-4 weeks

Crown fabrication

Root Canal Treatment (RCT)

Save your tooth with our advanced root canal treatments using latest technology and techniques.

Treatment Process

  1. Diagnosis: X-ray and examination
  2. Local Anesthesia: Complete pain management
  3. Pulp Removal: Clean infected pulp tissue
  4. Root Cleaning & Shaping: Prepare root canals
  5. Filling: Fill canals with biocompatible material
  6. Restoration: Place crown for protection

Duration & Timeline

60-90 minutes

Single sitting RCT

2-3 visits

Complex cases

1-2 weeks

Crown placement
