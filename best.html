Why Balaji Dental Clinic is the Best Dental Clinic in Jodhpur

February 5, 2026 Clinic

When it comes to choosing the best dental clinic in Jodhpur, patients have many options. However, Balaji Dental Clinic stands out as a premier destination for comprehensive, high-quality dental care. Here's why we're trusted by hundreds of families in Jodhpur.

Expert Team of Specialists

At Balaji Dental Clinic, we pride ourselves on having a team of highly qualified dental professionals. Our doctors hold prestigious MDS degrees and are gold medalists in their respective fields:

  • Dr. Suresh Kumar Sharma (MDS Orthodontics) - Specializing in cosmetic dentistry, digital dentistry, and advanced orthodontic treatments
  • Dr. S. Krishnan (MDS Periodontics) - Expert in gum surgeries and periodontal care with advanced regenerative techniques

This combination of expertise ensures that every patient receives treatment from the most qualified specialist for their specific needs.

State-of-the-Art Technology

We believe that modern dentistry requires modern equipment. Our clinic is equipped with:

  • ITERO Intraoral Scanner: For precise digital impressions and treatment planning

  • Advanced Digital Imaging: 3D CBCT scanning for accurate diagnosis
  • Latest Surgical Equipment: For safe and precise dental procedures

Comprehensive Treatment Options

Whether you need routine cleanings, advanced orthodontic treatment, dental implants, or cosmetic enhancements, we offer a complete range of services under one roof:

  • Preventive Care & Cleanings
  • Orthodontics (Braces & Invisalign)
  • Dental Implants & Prosthetics
  • Root Canal Treatment (Single Sitting)
  • Periodontal Surgery
  • Cosmetic Dentistry & Smile Design
  • Pediatric Dentistry

We understand that dental anxiety is common. Our clinic provides a welcoming, comfortable environment with:

  • Modern, hygienic facilities

  • Flexible appointment scheduling
